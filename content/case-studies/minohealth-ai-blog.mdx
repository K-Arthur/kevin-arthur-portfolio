Minohealth AI Labs is at the forefront of AI healthcare innovation in Africa. As their work gained international recognition, they needed a dedicated platform to communicate complex scientific findings to a diverse audience — from investors and potential partners to healthcare providers. I designed a content platform that achieved **2x the industry benchmark for reader engagement** while maintaining **full WCAG AA accessibility** across all devices.

---

## Business Constraint

**Bridging the gap between academic paper and blog post.** The content needed scientific rigor for peer review while remaining accessible to the general public.

- **Scientific papers** are dense, text-heavy, and intimidating
- **Marketing blogs** feel fluffy and lack substance
- **68% of the target audience** accessed content on mobile devices, particularly in African markets with varying internet conditions

<ReadabilityChart />

**Business goals:**
1. **Establish authority** — position Minohealth as a thought leader in African AI
2. **Improve readability** — create a distraction-free environment for long-form content
3. **Scalability** — design a system that grows with increasing publication output

---

## Strategic Approach

### 1. The "Paper-Like" Aesthetic

Chose a warm, off-white background (`FAF5EA`) with dark gray text to mimic reading a physical journal — reducing eye strain for long-form technical reading.

- **Headings:** Bold sans-serif in the brand's primary magenta — adds energy without compromising readability
- **Body:** High x-height sans-serif (Inter) optimized for screen reading at various sizes

<PaperToggle />

### 2. Distraction-Free Reading Layout

Single-column layout with no sidebar clutter, no trending posts, no ads in peripheral vision. Complex diagrams and code snippets get generous whitespace to breathe.

### 3. Clear Categorization System

Robust tagging system prominently displayed above article titles (RESEARCH, NEWS, COMPANY) — enabling visual scanning and immediate content-type identification.

### 4. Adaptive Light/Dark Modes

Seamless switching between viewing modes, ensuring reading comfort in any environment — critical for researchers working extended hours.
